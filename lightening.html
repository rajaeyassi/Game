Lightning Ronin

Lightning Ronin is a fierce and agile warrior in Elemental Fighter, wielding the power of lightning with unmatched skill. With lightning-fast reflexes and a razor-sharp katana, this enigmatic swordsman strikes down foes with precision and finesse. Cloaked in mystery and driven by a sense of justice, Lightning Ronin roams the land, delivering swift justice to those who threaten the balance of nature.
