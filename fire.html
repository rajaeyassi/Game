Fire Knight

Fire Knight is a formidable and relentless warrior in Elemental Fighter, harnessing the fiery power of the elements to vanquish enemies. Clad in armor adorned with flames and wielding a blazing sword, Fire Knight charges into battle with unyielding determination. With a heart burning with passion and a spirit as fierce as the flames they command, Fire Knight fights fiercely to protect the innocent and uphold honor. They leave a trail of scorched earth in their wake, a testament to their unstoppable strength and unwavering resolve.
